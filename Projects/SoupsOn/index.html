Joseph Babbitt is...

An Eagle Scout in the

Boy Scouts of America

For 8 years I was a member of Troop 662 in Manhattan, New York. During my time in the Boy Scouts I learned how to be an effective leader, complete tasks when faced with adversity, essential life skills, and of course, all the outdoor skills we're known for.

Over the course of my tenure with the troop I held multiple leadership positions, ranging from quartermaster all the way to Junior Assistant ScoutMaster.

As a part of the process of gaining the rank of Eagle, I had to plan, execute, and reflect upon a project of my choosing that would enrich my local community. In 2017, I worked with a senior center specializing in alzheimer's care. Working in tandem with their needs and my skillset, I created a computer program that automatically generated personalized weekly newsletters for all residents. Each newsletter was personally tailored based on a questionnaire and data generated by scout interviews with the residents. The program was implemented as deigned and currently services over 75 residents.

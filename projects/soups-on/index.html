Soups On

May 2019

Soup's on was created by my friend, Sam, and I at TeenHacks LI in the spring of 2019 in under 24 hours.

Another one of my utilitarian hackathon projects, I came up with the idea for Soup's On while working over the summer. My team would usually go out for lunch, but deciding where to go could be a conversation that evolves over hours. In hopes of keeping my notifications to a minimum, and giving myself one less decision to make.

The process is simple. Set up an account with google, add your friends, and tell us what restaurants you like. Then, every morning, sign in, answer a few quick questions and you're good to go. We'll pair you up with your friends that are in the mood for the same food and choose a restaurant you're all guaranteed to enjoy.
